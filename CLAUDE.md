# Career Materials — Job Search & Professional Development

## About This Project
Repository for job search materials: resumes, cover letters, pitch decks,
interview prep notes, and networking templates. Targeting VP AI Product Manager
roles and biotech industry positions.

## Key Files
- `resume/` — Master resume + role-specific variants
- `cover-letters/` — Templates and company-specific letters
- `pitch-decks/` — BiomeScope investor deck, personal brand deck
- `interview-prep/` — Company research, behavioral stories (STAR format), technical prep
- `networking/` — Outreach templates, follow-up sequences

## Standards
- All documents maintain consistent personal branding
- Quantify achievements: revenue %, user growth %, team size, etc.
- Key metrics to highlight: Ford Pro (20→325+ customers), Vacasa (33% engagement lift), PulteGroup (54% revenue growth)
- Tailor every cover letter — no generic submissions
- Keep resume to 2 pages max. Lead with impact.

## AI Assistance Rules
- When drafting content, match the tone of the target company's culture
- Always use active voice and strong action verbs
- Never fabricate metrics or experience — only reframe existing achievements
- For interview prep: generate likely questions based on job description + company research
